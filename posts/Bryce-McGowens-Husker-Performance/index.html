Bryce McGowens: Not the Husker He Should Have Been

basketball
huskers
NCAA
sports
Author

Kate Cordes

Published

May 6, 2023

Bryce McGowens was the first and only 5-star recruit Husker Hoops has ever landed. He was the player destined to save Fred Hoiberg’s tenure at Nebraska. While Hoiberg has stuck around in Lincoln, it is not due to the 2020-2021 season. Instead, the season was a disappointment, as McGowens carried the Cornhuskers to a pitiful 10-22 season record.
